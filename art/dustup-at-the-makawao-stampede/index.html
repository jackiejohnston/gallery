Title:

Dustup at the Makawao Stampede
Artist: Jackie Johnston
Info: I enjoy the bewilderment of my mainland friends when I tell them about going to rodeo events in Hawaii and the fact that cowboys (paniolos) have been wrangling cattle in Hawaii longer than in any of the western states. I took the reference photo for this during the team roping qualifications for the 4th of July Makawao Stampede Rodeo at the Oskie Rice Arena in 2023.
About: Original
